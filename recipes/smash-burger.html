How to make a smash burger:

Ingredients:

For the Smash Burger:

  • 250g (½ lb) ground beef (80/20 fat ratio for juiciness)
  • 2 burger buns
  • 2 slices cheese (cheddar or American)
  • ½ small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp butter (for toasting buns)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil (for cooking)
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

For the Sauce

  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika

For the Fries:

  • 2 medium potatoes, cut into thin strips
  • 2 cups cold water
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch (optional, for crispiness)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Fries:
    1. Soak potatoes in cold water for 30 minutes (removes excess starch for crispiness).
    2. Drain, pat dry, and toss with cornstarch.
    3. Heat oil to 350°F (175°C) and fry for 3-4 minutes until golden.
    4. Remove, drain, and season with salt.
  2. Make the Sauce:
    1. Mix mayo, ketchup, mustard, garlic powder, and paprika. Set aside.
  3. Cook the smash Burger:
    1. Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat and lightly oil it.
    2. Divide the beef into two loose balls (don’t overwork).
    3. Place a ball on the skillet and smash it down using a spatula until thin.
    4. Season with salt and pepper. Let it cook 2 minutes without moving.
    5. Flip, add cheese on top, and cook for 1 more minute until melted.
  4. Assemble & Serve:
    1. Toast buns in butter.
    2. Spread sauce on the bottom bun, add the patty, chopped onions, and top bun.
    3. Serve with fries and enjoy!
