How To Make Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 12 oven-ready lasagna noodles (no boiling required)
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (or Italian sausage)
  • 1 jar (24 oz) of marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brown the meat: In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat, then stir in the marinara sauce. Simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Prepare the cheese mixture: In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. Assemble the lasagna:
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    • Layer 3 lasagna noodles on top.
    • Spread ⅓ of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    • Sprinkle some mozzarella cheese.
    • Repeat layers until you run out of ingredients, finishing with mozzarella on top.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and slightly golden.
  6. Rest & Serve: Let it sit for 5-10 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
